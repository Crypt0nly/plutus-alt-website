      How it works

      From goal to done.

      No new workflow to learn. Describe the outcome — Plutus handles everything in between.

      1

      Just say what you want.

      “Research our top 3 competitors”, “clean up the open PRs”, “plan Thursday’s launch” — plain language is the whole interface.

      2

      It turns your goal into a plan.

      Plutus breaks a fuzzy request into concrete, checkable steps — and works out what needs to happen in what order before it touches anything.

      3

      It does the work in your tools.

      Drafts emails, organizes files, writes and commits code, books time — directly inside Gmail, Calendar, GitHub, Discord and more. No copy-paste required.

      4

      You see exactly what changed.

      Every run ends with a clear summary: what Plutus did, where it did it, and what’s worth a second look.

      Features

      Everything you’d expect.

      It already knows how you work.

      Projects, preferences, contacts and reusable routines live in one workspace. Plutus remembers — so you don’t repeat yourself.

      Real output.

      Results land back in the right tools — not in yet another inbox.

      Team-ready.

      Share knowledge and routines, review each other’s work, stay in control.

      Cloud or local.

      Start instantly in the cloud, or run it on your machine for private work.

      Integrations
